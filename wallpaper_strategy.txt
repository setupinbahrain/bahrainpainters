5.3 Internal Linking Architecture
The wallpaper content silo follows a hub-and-spoke model:
	•	Hub Page (/wallpaper-bahrain) links DOWN to all 10 sub-pages
	•	Each sub-page links UP to the hub and ACROSS to 2-3 related sub-pages
	•	Blog posts link to the most relevant service page (e.g., '3D Wallpaper Guide' blog links to /3d-wallpaper-bahrain)
	•	Painting service pages cross-link to wallpaper pages where relevant (e.g., 'Interior Painting' links to 'Accent Wall Design')
	•	Location pages (Manama, Riffa, Muharraq) mention both painting AND wallpaper services
Cross-Silo Linking: The painting pillar (/painting-services-bahrain) and wallpaper pillar (/wallpaper-bahrain) should link to each other. This creates topical authority across the entire 'wall finishing' space in Google's eyes.

9. Technical SEO: Schema Markup for Wallpaper Pages
Each wallpaper page should implement these structured data schemas to maximize rich snippet visibility:

	•	LocalBusiness Schema: Include wallpaper-specific service types, service area (all of Bahrain), and price range indicators
	•	Service Schema: One per wallpaper service type (installation, removal, 3D, vinyl, custom, mural, commercial)
	•	FAQ Schema: Add 5-8 FAQs per page targeting 'People Also Ask' boxes in Google
	•	Product Schema: For specific wallpaper products/types with pricing, availability, brand
	•	HowTo Schema: For process pages (installation steps, removal guide) to win featured snippets
	•	Review/AggregateRating Schema: Display star ratings in search results
	•	BreadcrumbList Schema: Home > Services > Wallpaper > [Specific Type] for clear navigation in SERPs

Implementation Note for Antigravity: All schema should be implemented as JSON-LD in the <head> of each page. Use Google's Rich Results Test to validate before going live.
